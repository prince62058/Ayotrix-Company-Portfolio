Backend Requirements
Every form should submit to a separate endpoint or include a hidden serviceType/productType field (e.g., ecommerce-development, google-ads, seo).
Store all enquiries in the database with:
Form Type
Service/Product Name
Submission Date & Time
User Details
Source URL
Send confirmation email to the user.
Send notification email to the admin.
Add spam protection (Google reCAPTCHA or Cloudflare Turnstile).
Validate all required fields.
Show success/error messages with smooth animations.

Result: Har page ka apna customized enquiry form hoga, jisse leads properly categorize hongi aur sales team ko turant pata chal jayega ki enquiry kis service ya product ke liye aayi hai.
